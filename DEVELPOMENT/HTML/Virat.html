Virat Kohli: The King of Cricket

Explore the remarkable achievements of one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history.

ODI Records

Virat Kohli holds the record for the fastest century in ODIs (off 52 balls against Australia in 2013). He has scored over 12,000 runs in ODIs, with an average of over 58.

Test Cricket

As of 2023, Kohli has 27 Test centuries, the most by any Indian batsman. He has led India to numerous victories and is known for his consistency.

T20 World Cup

Kohli captained India to victory in the 2021 T20 World Cup, scoring 488 runs in the tournament, including a memorable 82* in the final.

Captaincy

Under his leadership, India became the No. 1 Test team in 2016. He has won multiple series and is praised for his aggressive captaincy style.

Individual Awards

Kohli has won the ICC ODI Player of the Year multiple times (2012, 2017, 2018). He is also the recipient of the Arjuna Award and Padma Shri.

Brand and Influence

Beyond cricket, Kohli is a global icon with millions of followers on social media. He has endorsements with major brands and advocates for fitness and mental health.
